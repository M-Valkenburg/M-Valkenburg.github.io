Nature

If you’re looking to learn more about and get in touch with nature, you can’t really go wrong almost anywhere you go in the state. There’s so much to learn practically everywhere, but just a couple of the places to consider are the 46,000-acre Timucuan Ecological & Historic Preserve and the 54,000-acre Kissimmee Prairie Preserve State Park. Also, no Florida nature list would be complete without mentioning the massive 1.5-million-acre Everglades National Park.
